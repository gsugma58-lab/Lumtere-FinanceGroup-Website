Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility

You must be 18 years or older and a resident of Papua New Guinea to apply for financing through Lumtere Finance All applications must be for business purposes only. We may perform credit checks as part of our assessment process.

2. Employment Types

Employed applicants: Must provide the last 2 months of pay slips. These will be verified before approval.

Self-employed applicants: Must provide details of collateral (property, vehicle, or other assets) with a minimum value ofK1000 Proof of ownership may be required.

3. Collateral Requirements

For secured loans, acceptable collateral includes:

  • Property (residential or commercial)
  • Vehicles (under 10 years old)
  • Business equipment
  • Other valuable assets (by agreement)

4. Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously. All information submitted through our application form is encrypted and stored securely. We will never share your data with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

5. Application Process

By submitting an application, you confirm that all information provided is true and accurate. False information may result in immediate rejection and could affect future applications.

6. Agreement

Submitting this form constitutes an agreement that we may contact you regarding your application via phone, email, or post. It does not constitute a binding offer of finance.
