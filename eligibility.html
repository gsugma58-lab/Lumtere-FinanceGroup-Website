Quick document & eligibility checklist

Based on MiBank (EsiLoan / Salary Loan) & Wantok Finance criteria — simple, transparent.
Income & employment
  • Minimum 6 months employment MiBank EsiLoan
  • Regular wage/salary earner Salary Loan
  • HR/Payroll letter (employment confirmation) Wantok
ID & personal docs
  • Two valid IDs: NID, passport, driver's licence, work ID, Super ID
  • Clear photo-bearing ID (MiBank EsiLoan)
  • Completed & signed application form
Bank account
  • Salary account statements – last 2 months (Wantok)
  • MiBank savings account required (Salary Loan)
  • 2–3 months bank statements (general)
Proof of income
  • 2 most recent payslips (3 if overtime) – MiBank EsiLoan
  • Payslips for Wantok (via employer)
Employment verification
  • Employment confirmation letter (start date) – MiBank new clients
  • HR/Payroll letter for Wantok Finance
Credit checks
  • Statements of all existing loans (Wantok)
  • Refinancing statements if applicable (MiBank)
Collateral (if needed)
  • MiBank Speed Loan: savings / fixed deposit to cover exposure
  • Wantok: usually employer‑backed, salary deduction
Additional documents
  • Signed Statutory Declaration (Wantok)
  • Irrevocable Salary Deduction Authority (MiBank EsiLoan)
  • Bank statements 2–3 months
Other conditions
  • Notify MiBank if employment/address changes
  • Wantok: original documents with stamp (no plain copies)

Printable checklist summary

Personal • 2 valid photo IDs
• completed app form
• passport photo (if required)

Employment • HR letter • 6+ months continuous • 3 latest payslips (or 2 if no overtime)

Income & banking • 2–3 months salary statements • active MiBank/Wantok salary account • ISDA if required

Financial checks • statements of existing loans • no undisclosed debts • statutory declaration (Wantok)

Collateral • savings/fixed deposit for MiBank Speed Loan

lender-specific marks: MiBank EsiLoan / Salary Loan & Wantok Finance — always verify latest criteria.
